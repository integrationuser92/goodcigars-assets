GoodCigars Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-08

Summary

GoodCigars is designed to work without user accounts and without advertising or analytics SDKs.

Data Collection

  • GoodCigars does not collect personal data on our servers
  • GoodCigars does not use advertising SDKs
  • GoodCigars does not track users across apps or websites

On-Device Data

GoodCigars stores your cigar library, reviews, smoke sessions, humidor inventory, and optional photos locally on your device.

Camera Access

If you grant camera permission, the app uses it only to capture photos for cigar and humidor records.

Delete Data

You can delete all local data at any time inside the app from Settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact dimarkak@gmail.com.
