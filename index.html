GoodC

Private collection tracking for iPhone.

GoodC helps you track your personal library, storage, notes, ratings, and optional photos in a local-first app.

  • No account or sign in required
  • Data stays on-device
  • Optional camera access only for record photos
  • Optional catalog import from URL

Sample catalog URL: https://integrationuser92.github.io/goodc-assets/catalog-index.json
